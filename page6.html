Monthly Sales Year by Year

Total Sum of Sales was higher for 2020 (10,01,124.79) than 2019 (5,64,679.54).

December in Year 2020 made up 10.61% of Sum of Sales.

Average Sum of Sales was higher for 2020 (83,427.07) than 2019 (47,056.63).

Sum of Sales for 2020 and 2019 diverged the most when the Month was December, when 2020 were 87,786.81 higher than 2019.

Monthly Profit Year by Year

December in Year 2019 made up 10.20% of Sum of Profit.

Limitations

One of the limitations of this particular dataset or Visualisation is that it can be used to analyse super store sales only in America and can only be used to make decisions in America. Other Super Stores might need different dataset belonging to particular region or country
